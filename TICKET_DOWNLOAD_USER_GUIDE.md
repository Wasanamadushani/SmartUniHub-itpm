# Ticket Download Feature - User Guide

## 🎫 How to Download Your Event Tickets

### Step 1: Book Your Seats
1. Navigate to the event booking page
2. Select the number of seats you want (e.g., 5 seats)
3. Fill in your details and payment information
4. Submit your booking

### Step 2: Wait for Payment Approval
After submitting your payment:
- Your payment status will show as **"Pending Verification"**
- An admin will review your payment receipt
- The page automatically refreshes every 3 seconds to check for approval
- You'll see a message: "Admin is verifying your payment..."

### Step 3: Payment Approved! 🎉
Once the admin approves your payment:
- Status changes to **"Approved"**
- A green checkmark (✓) appears
- The page displays: "Your payment has been verified and approved"
- **Download Tickets button appears**

### Step 4: Download Your Tickets
1. Click the **"🎫 Download X Tickets"** button
2. The system will download individual tickets for each seat
3. If you booked 5 seats, you'll get 5 separate HTML files:
   - `ticket-1-TKT-ABC123-1.html`
   - `ticket-2-TKT-ABC123-2.html`
   - `ticket-3-TKT-ABC123-3.html`
   - `ticket-4-TKT-ABC123-4.html`
   - `ticket-5-TKT-ABC123-5.html`

### Step 5: View and Print Your Tickets
1. Open each HTML file in your web browser
2. Each ticket shows:
   - **Event name and details**
   - **Your seat number** (Seat #1, Seat #2, etc.)
   - **Unique ticket number** for verification
   - **Event location and date/time**
   - **Your name and email**
   - **QR code** for entry scanning
3. Print the tickets or save them as PDFs
4. You can also view them on your mobile device

## 📱 What Each Ticket Contains

### Header Section
- Event title
- Seat number badge (e.g., "Seat #1")

### Ticket Information
- **Ticket Number**: Unique identifier (e.g., TKT-ABC123-1)
- **Seat Number**: Your assigned seat (#1, #2, etc.)
- **Attendee Name**: Your name
- **Email**: Your email address
- **Event Location**: Where the event takes place
- **Event Date**: Date of the event
- **Start Time**: When the event begins
- **End Time**: When the event ends

### QR Code Section
- QR code for entry verification
- Ticket number in text format
- "Scan QR Code at Entry" instruction

### Footer
- Important reminder to bring the ticket
- Verification timestamp
- Ticket count (e.g., "Ticket 1 of 5")

## 💡 Tips and Best Practices

### For Digital Tickets
- Save all ticket files in a dedicated folder
- Name the folder with the event name and date
- Keep tickets on your phone for easy access
- Take screenshots as backup

### For Printed Tickets
- Print all tickets before the event
- Use good quality paper
- Print in color for better QR code scanning
- Keep tickets in a safe place

### At the Event
- Bring all tickets (one per person)
- Arrive early to avoid queues
- Have tickets ready for scanning
- Each person needs their own ticket

### Sharing Tickets
- If you booked for multiple people, share the tickets
- Send each person their specific ticket file
- Make sure they know their seat number
- Remind them to bring the ticket (printed or digital)

## ❓ Frequently Asked Questions

### Q: Can I download tickets before payment approval?
**A:** No, tickets are only available after admin approves your payment.

### Q: What if I lose my ticket files?
**A:** You can return to the payment success page and download them again. The link is also available in your dashboard.

### Q: Can I print multiple copies of the same ticket?
**A:** Yes, but each ticket can only be used once for entry. Don't share the same ticket with multiple people.

### Q: Do I need all tickets if I booked for a group?
**A:** Yes, you need one ticket per person. If you booked 5 seats, you need all 5 tickets for entry.

### Q: Can I view tickets on my phone?
**A:** Yes! Open the HTML files in your mobile browser. They're responsive and mobile-friendly.

### Q: What if the QR code doesn't scan?
**A:** Event staff can manually verify using your ticket number shown on the ticket.

### Q: Can I transfer my ticket to someone else?
**A:** Currently, tickets are non-transferable. Contact support if you need to change attendee details.

### Q: How do I know which seat is mine?
**A:** Each ticket clearly shows "Seat #X" at the top. Seats are numbered 1, 2, 3, etc.
